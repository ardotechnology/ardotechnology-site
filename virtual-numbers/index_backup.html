Can I use a Mexico virtual number for WhatsApp Business?

Yes! Our Mexico virtual numbers are perfect for WhatsApp Business verification. You can use the number for your WhatsApp Business account to communicate with Mexican customers. Note: Local Mexico numbers don't receive SMS, but WhatsApp verification works through voice call.

Do I need a physical office in Mexico to get a number?

No physical office required! You can get a Mexico virtual number from anywhere in the US. This is perfect for establishing local presence without the cost of opening a physical office in Mexico.

How quickly can I get my Mexico number activated?

Most Mexico numbers are activated within 24 hours. Some cities may take up to 48 hours. We'll keep you updated throughout the activation process.

Can I forward calls to my US phone number?

Absolutely! You can forward calls from your Mexico number to any US phone number, mobile phone, VoIP phone, or even multiple numbers simultaneously. No additional international fees.

Are these numbers compliant with Mexican regulations?

Yes, all our Mexico virtual numbers are fully compliant with IFT (Instituto Federal de Telecomunicaciones) regulations. We work with licensed Mexican carriers to ensure legal and reliable service.

Can I get numbers in multiple Mexican cities?

Yes! You can have virtual numbers in as many Mexican cities as you need. This is perfect for businesses serving multiple regions or wanting to test different markets.

What's the difference between local and toll-free Mexico numbers?

Local numbers (LADA codes like 55, 81, 33) are specific to cities and build local trust. Toll-free numbers (01-800) are free for callers anywhere in Mexico and project a national presence. Both can forward to your US office.

No long-term contracts required! All plans are month-to-month and you can cancel anytime. We also offer a 30-day money-back guarantee.
